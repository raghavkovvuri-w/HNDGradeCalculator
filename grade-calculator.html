HNC/HND Digital Technologies

Grade Calculator & Assessment Tool

Important Information

  • HNC Qualification: Overall grade is calculated using all Level 4 units (120 credits total)
  • HND Qualification: Overall grade is calculated using Level 5 units only (120 credits total)
  • Credit Value: Each unit is worth 15 credits
  • Compensated Units: Units with U grade earn 0 points but can count toward credit requirements (maximum one 15-credit unit)
  • Programme Structure: Choose your pathway (Software Development or Cyber Security) and qualification level to begin

Grade Points Reference

Your overall qualification grade is determined by the total points accumulated across all units. Each grade level corresponds to specific point values per credit, which are then multiplied by the 15 credits per unit.

Pass 4 points per credit
60 points per unit
(Satisfactory achievement)
Merit 6 points per credit
90 points per unit
(High quality work)
Distinction 8 points per credit
120 points per unit
(Outstanding achievement)

Overall Qualification Grade Thresholds

Pass 420 - 599 points
Merit 600 - 839 points
Distinction 840+ points
